# Overview

> This workspace is a skill-first knowledge workspace maintained on the local filesystem. See [[CLAUDE]] for the workflow rules.

Use this wiki as a maintained knowledge layer:
- capture new sources into `raw/sources/`
- ingest them into `wiki/` pages
- query the wiki first
- save valuable answers back into the workspace
- review and correct pages over time

This page should eventually summarize the major themes, entities, and open questions in the workspace.
